Welcome

3-steps quick how-to:

1. Add

Add folder(s) to be proceed by pimouss by right-clicking on the Project dock. You will have to choose a folder on your disk and give this pimous a name

2. Adjust & Run!

Edit/change pimouss option by clicking on the config button. Launch process by clicking on the HTML-ize button.

3. Control & Preview

Look at log for errors in the Log dock. Preview generated HTML by right-clicking on the html file in the Report dock.
